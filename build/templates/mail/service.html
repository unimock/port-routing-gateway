You are registered as a service-user at the service-gateway TEMPLATE_HOSTNAME
This enables you to establish port forwarding to different customer systems.

To configure local port forwarding to the service-gateway,
copy the attached file into your ~/.ssh/ directory and
append Include config.* to your existing ~/.ssh/config:

echo 'Include config.*' >> ~/.ssh/config

To establish local port forwarding to the customer system, enter:

ssh ‹custom-name›

Here you will find further information.
As soon as you exit the console with Ctrl + C the local port forwarding will be terminated.

You find the supported ‹custom-name› with:

grep "^Host " ~/.ssh/TEMPLATE_SSH_CONFIG
